Personal Auto Grading Roadmap

Thu Jun 21, 2018 by in  jupyter, education, grading

I’m doing a bunch of autograding work in the Jupyter ecosystem. I’ve little direct experience in this vast area, which makes building good systems for it hard. When I have explicit users with specific use cases to meet, it always becomes easier. This blog post is an attempt to:

  1. Explain the use cases & users I need to serve
  2. Describe the components I am working on

As usual, I am attempting to build these within the larger Jupyter community in ways everyone can use, rather than just the amazing folks at Berkeley.

Use cases

I’ve two primary use cases, at fairly extreme ends!

  1. This EdX course, with several thousand active students served by a small number of instructors
  2. Connector courses at UC Berkeley. There are quite a few of them, serving a smallish number of students. Faculty who are experts in their fields but not at navigating software development tools teach them.
