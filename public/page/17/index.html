How to increase Cheese’s Burst Mode Photo Count

2010-11-07
code

“Why does Cheese have a maximum photo count of 100 in burst mode?” questions have popped up quite a few times on the cheese list. It is just a teeny, tiny bug – fixed in master. The work around is simple: Till Cheese 2.32 Raise it to whatever value you want in line 8 of /usr/share/cheese/cheese-prefs.ui. (If you don’t know what version you are running, check Help -> About) (Fix is just raising the limit from 100 to something that most people will likely not hit.
