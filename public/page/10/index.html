Calculating the population of India on Wikipedia

2013-03-31
links > wiki

Exploring the English Wikipedia randomly, I encountered this template used to approximate the current live population of India. It uses the following simple formula: (1176242 + 42.197260273969 * days_since(2010,1,26)) * 000 It’s based on population at a specific data + projected growth. The ‘update’ is done by (best guess) some bot hitting action=purge every night to re-do the calculation. And where is this used? After looking at the template’s info page, the only article this seems to be used in is the India article, and that too only for population density statistics, rather than actual population statistics.
